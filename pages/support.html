Technical

The SPT-NXT records at 10Hz (10 readings per second) — the same frequency used by Catapult and STATSports in professional sport. GPS accuracy is typically within 1-2 metres in open-sky conditions. The device also includes an accelerometer and gyroscope for player load calculations.
