About SPT

Our Story

Designed by Players, for Players

SPT was born from a simple frustration — professional-grade GPS tracking cost tens of thousands of dollars, putting it out of reach for 99% of teams. We set out to change that.

The Problem

Elite GPS tracking systems like Catapult cost $50,000+ per team. STATSports starts at $5,000+. For grassroots, amateur and semi-professional teams — the vast majority of team sports worldwide — this was simply unaffordable. Coaches knew GPS data could transform their team, but they could not access it.

Our Solution

SPT delivers 10Hz GPS accuracy — the same frequency used by professional teams — at a fraction of the price. No subscriptions. No contracts. No hidden fees. Buy the device, put it on, and start getting data. GameTraka analytics is included free, forever.

K-Sport Acquisition

In 2023, SPT acquired K-Sport — a pioneer in wearable GPS technology with over a decade of R&D. This gave SPT access to world-class hardware engineering, validated sensor technology, and deep expertise in sports performance tracking. The result: the SPT-NXT — our most advanced device yet.
